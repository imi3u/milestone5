Frequently Asked Questions

What is DevBoard?

DevBoard is a task management platform that helps developers track and complete tasks efficiently.

How do I mark a task as completed?

Click the "Completed" button on any task to mark it as done and update your activity log.

Can I reset all tasks?

Currently, tasks can only be completed. A reset feature is planned for future updates.

How often does the date update?

The date updates every second to reflect the real-time Bangladesh time zone.

Where can I report a bug?

You can report bugs via our feedback form or contact support at support@devboard.com.
